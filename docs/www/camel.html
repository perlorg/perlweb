The Camel has been the symbol of Perl since its appearance on the cover of the first edition of Programming Perl. The camel with the topic of Perl is a trademark of O’Reilly Media, Inc. Used with permission.

O'Reilly confirmed that Perl cannot use the specific camel image from the Programming Perl book, but a new camel image could be used. The key restriction is that no other publisher can use a camel on the cover of a book about Perl,

Starting in Dec 2024, a new Perl camel began to be used. The home for this is https://github.com/metacpan/perl-assets
