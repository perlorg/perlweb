About the website

The current look of www.perl.org was designed by Leo Lapworth, updating the six year old previous design. Robert Spier and Ask Bjoern Hansen are the webmasters and sysadmins. We believe in browser independence and accessibility, but not IE6. Our center of operations is The Perl NOC. You can track our progress at the Perl NOC Log. The souce for the site is stored in github.

When you need to link to Perl, we want you to think perl.org
