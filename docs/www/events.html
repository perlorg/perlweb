YAPC

  • Yet Another Perl Conference (YAPC) is a series of popular annual conferences with roots in the Perl user community.
  • Annual conferences in North America and and Europe (and other continents, see the YAPC site for more).
  • An inexpensive alternative to other conferences.
  • A friendly venue for sharing research, learning new techniques, and talking with leading developers.
  • YAPC Survey results - see the breakdown of attendees and feedback about their experiences at YAPC conferences

The Perl Conference

  • An annual event since 1997.
  • Now part of the O'Reilly Open Source Convention.
  • Tutorials, technical presentations, social events, and more.
